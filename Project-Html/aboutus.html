About Us

Honda Cars India Ltd. (HCIL) is a leading automobile manufacturer in India and a wholly-owned subsidiary of Honda Motor Co., Japan. Honda entered the Indian automobile market in 1995 and has since been committed to delivering high-quality, reliable, and fuel-efficient vehicles. With a strong focus on innovation, safety, and customer satisfaction, Honda Cars India offers a wide range of premium sedans and SUVs designed to meet the needs of Indian customers.


Our Vision

To be a company that society wants to exist by creating mobility solutions that enrich people’s lives and contribute to a sustainable future.

Our Mission

To deliver superior products and services through advanced technology, environmental responsibility, and customer-centric values.


Our Core Values

  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Quality & Reliability
  • Innovation & Technology
  • Environmental Responsibility
  • Safety First
